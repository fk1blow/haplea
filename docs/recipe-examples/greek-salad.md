# Greek Salad

Fresh Mediterranean salad with feta and olives.

## category

salads

## tags

greek, mediterranean, vegetarian, healthy, quick, summer, no-cook

## ingredients

- 2 large cucumbers, chopped into chunks
- 4 ripe tomatoes, chopped into chunks
- 1 bell pepper, chopped into chunks
- 1 red onion, thinly sliced
- 1 cup kalamata olives
- 200g feta cheese, cubed
- 3 tbsp olive oil
- 1 tbsp red wine vinegar
- 1 tsp dried oregano
- Salt and pepper to taste

## instructions

Chop cucumbers, tomatoes, and bell peppers into chunks. Slice red onion thinly. Combine in bowl with kalamata olives. Add cubed feta cheese. Dress with olive oil, red wine vinegar, dried oregano, salt, and pepper. Toss gently.

## notes

Best when tomatoes are in season. Let it sit for 15 minutes before serving for flavors to develop.
