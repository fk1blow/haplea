# Fresh Guacamole

Creamy, zesty avocado dip perfect for any occasion.

## category

## tags

...

## instructions

Mash ripe avocados in a bowl, leaving some chunks. Mix in diced tomatoes, finely chopped red onion, cilantro, lime juice, and salt. Taste and adjust seasoning. Let sit for 10 minutes to let flavors meld.

## notes

Press plastic wrap directly onto the surface to prevent browning. Best served within 2 hours of making.
